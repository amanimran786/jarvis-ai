# Story Bank Template

Adapted for Jarvis from the open-source Career-Ops project by santifer:
https://github.com/santifer/career-ops

This file is the structure Jarvis should use when helping Aman build reusable interview stories.

## Goal

Maintain five to ten deep STAR plus reflection stories that can flex across many behavioral questions.

Instead of memorizing one answer per question, keep a small library of high-signal stories.

## Suggested Structure

### [Theme] Story Title

Source:
Company, project, or role where the story happened

Situation:
What was happening and why it mattered

Task:
What Aman was responsible for

Action:
What Aman actually did, specifically

Result:
What changed, including measurable impact when possible

Reflection:
What Aman learned, what he would do differently, or what principle he now uses

Best for questions about:
List the question types this story answers well

## Good Story Themes

- leadership under ambiguity
- conflict resolution
- failure and recovery
- process improvement
- cross-functional influence
- technical debugging
- quality or safety improvement
- customer or user impact
- ownership beyond role scope

## Story-Bank Rules

- prefer real measurable outcomes
- keep the action section concrete and personal
- do not over-polish into something that sounds fake
- a reflection is required for high-value behavioral stories
- one strong story should be reusable across multiple prompts
