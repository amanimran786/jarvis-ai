# Candidate Profile Template

Adapted for Jarvis from the open-source Career-Ops project by santifer:
https://github.com/santifer/career-ops

Use this structure when Jarvis helps Aman define or refine his career-search profile.

## Candidate

- full name
- email
- phone
- location
- LinkedIn
- portfolio or project site
- GitHub

## Target Roles

List the real target roles Jarvis should optimize for.

Include:

- primary roles
- adjacent roles
- role archetypes
- level targets

Good examples:

- AI Platform / LLMOps
- Technical AI PM
- AI Solutions Architect
- Forward Deployed Engineer
- Trust and Safety / AI Safety
- Security operations with engineering depth

## Narrative

Capture:

- one-line headline
- exit story
- top strengths or superpowers
- proof points with measurable outcomes

Good proof points include:

- major launches
- measurable quality improvements
- detection or enforcement wins
- automation or tooling wins
- leadership under ambiguity

## Compensation

Track:

- target range
- minimum acceptable range
- preferred comp structure
- flexibility

## Location

Track:

- country
- city
- timezone
- visa or sponsorship status
- remote versus hybrid constraints
- travel or onsite flexibility

## Why This Matters

Jarvis should use this profile as stable career context, so company-specific prep and role-fit answers do not reset to generic interview advice every time.
