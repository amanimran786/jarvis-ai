## Story Selection Rules

- Pick one anchor story and one supporting story before adding a third.
- Match the story to the role's risk model, not just to matching keywords.
- For leadership roles, lead with scope and operating judgment before technical detail.
- For AI safety roles, lead with risk interpretation and scalable controls before general operations polish.
- For policy roles, lead with tradeoffs and consistency before tool-building.
