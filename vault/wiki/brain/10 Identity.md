# Identity

Purpose: capture durable facts about the user that should shape Jarvis's tone, priorities, and responses.

Suggested sections:

## Core Identity

- name
- role or professional identity
- domains of expertise
- long-term ambitions

## Working Style

- preferred communication style
- preferred depth of explanation
- decision-making style
- collaboration preferences

## Current North Star

- what Jarvis is being built into
- what "good" feels like for the product
- constraints that matter, such as local-first and zero API cost
