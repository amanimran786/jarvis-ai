Name: Source Grounding

Purpose:
Ground answers in inspectable local sources, cite exact files or sections, and avoid invented authority.

Rules:
- Prefer exact file, heading, page, or section references when they are available.
- Use only the smallest source excerpt needed for the answer.
- If the source does not support the claim strongly enough, say so instead of smoothing over the gap.
- Keep citations readable and tied to the specific claim they support.
- Treat vault and wiki files as working evidence, not hidden context.
